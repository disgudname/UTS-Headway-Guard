Headway Diagnostics

Fetches the current headway tracker settings, stop approach angles, cached arrival/departure state, and the most recent logged events so you can paste them into a ticket or text file.

Stop association drops

Most recent snapshots that could not be tied to a stop. Route mismatches are highlighted.

Arrival suppressions

Recent arrival attempts that were ignored (duplicate arrivals or vehicles that never left the previous stop).

Recent snapshot decisions

Last few vehicle snapshots with their nearest stop match, distance, and whether an arrival or departure was recorded.

Vehicle name cache

Snapshot of the vehicle ID → vehicle name lookup the headway tracker has cached, plus the most recent events where we could not attach a vehicle name.
